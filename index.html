Everything you own, on track

Real features, no fluff — this is exactly what the app does.

Track it your way

By date, by distance (km), by both — whichever comes first — or by fuel average. Every item measured by what actually matters for it.

Smart reminders

Get notified days or weeks ahead, on the due date, and escalating nudges if something goes overdue. Delivered as reliable push notifications.

Recurring tasks

Set something to repeat — every few months, yearly, or every X kilometres — and keepr rolls it over to the next cycle automatically when you're done.

Costs & reports

Log what each service costs and see totals by month, half-year, or year. Trends and charts break spending down by task, category, or type.

Organise by category

Group items as Vehicle, Appliance, or Personal — or create your own categories to match how you think about your stuff.

No ads, no tracking

Clean, fast, distraction-free — with light and dark themes. Your data is tied to your account and there when you need it.
